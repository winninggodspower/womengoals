About Us

Women Goals is a Non governmental organisation aimed to help the female gender recognise their place in the society, help them become who they dream of becoming and finally assisting them in the little way we can. We are a volunteer group that focuses on empowering the girl child, women with the right tools to becoming self reliant through increased knowledge and skill This platform covers a whole lot of humanitarian services such as outreach, donations and educative events.

Aims / Objections

Hope

Bringing hope to underprivileged Females in our society.

Female Education

To provide underprivileged female children with a well-rounded education.

Community Service

To increase the awareness of the female child and of the need to participate in community service initiatives.

Leadership

To raise a generation of change agents/nation builders with good leadership qualities.

Mentorship

To provide valuable and life changing mentorship to children in general both male and female.

Skill Acquisition

To train as many females as we can to be independent by skill acquisition
