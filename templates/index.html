About Us

Women Goals is a Non governmental organisation aimed to help the female gender recognise their place in the society, help them become who they dream of becoming and finally assisting them in the little way we can. We are a volunteer group that focuses on empowering the girl child, women with the right tools to becoming self reliant through increased knowledge and skill This platform covers a whole lot of humanitarian services such as outreach, donations and educative events.

Aims / Objections

Hope

Bringing hope to underprivileged Females in our society.

Female Education

To provide underprivileged female children with a well-rounded education.

Community Service

To increase the awareness of the female child and of the need to participate in community service initiatives.

Leadership

To raise a generation of change agents/nation builders with good leadership qualities.

Mentorship

To provide valuable and life changing mentorship to children in general both male and female.

Skill Acquisition

To train as many females as we can to be independent by skill acquisition

Vision / Mission

To ensure every Girl/Woman is living their best life, achieving their dreams.
Empowering the Girl child and Women with the right tools to becoming self reliant through increased knowledge and skills

Projects / activities

IG GIRL TALK

The IG GIRL TALK series holds on our live instagram page, where we talk about our society at large and the negative and positive influence it has on the female gender. We also talk on general impactful topics. so far we have had two series
(series 1.0 and 2.0).

CELEBRATION OF THE GIRL CHILD DAY 2018 (DELTA STATE, WARRI.)

As part of our goals we have chosen to always celebrate the girl child with the internationally recognized date. having a fun field and insighful event and giving out major writting materials, like books, pen and mathset. Okpaka Secondary School, Warri, Delta State was our first visit and event ever held. we gave wriiting mareials to 200 hundred students and talked to over 500 students in Jss 1 and 2.
